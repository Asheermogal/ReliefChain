How It Works

Step 1: Connect Wallet

Connect your Ethereum wallet (e.g., MetaMask) to interact with the ReliefChain smart contract.

Step 2: Buy Tokens

Purchase relief tokens using your connected wallet. Choose from available token packages and specify the number of tokens you want to buy.

Step 3: Distribution

The purchased tokens are securely stored in the ReliefChain smart contract and are transparently distributed to affected communities based on predefined criteria.
